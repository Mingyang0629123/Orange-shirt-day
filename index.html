What are residential school


Residential schools were made from a project called the Canadian Indian Residential School System, it was sponsored by the goverment. Their goal was to get rid indigenous people culture and change it to Canadian culture.































To do this many kids were forced into residential school. Their parent's being threaten if they do not. They were captured by bus and sended to a residential school

Third question: In these residential schools they couldn't speak their own languages, they couldn't practice their culture and they couldn't use their real name. And they were punished badly if they broke any of these rules.

On September 30, we celebrate orange shirt day to spread awareness and stories about the tragedy of residential school and also to make sure this incident never will happen again forever.
